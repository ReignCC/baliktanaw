Local Delicacies

Batangas Coffee

Known for its rich aroma and bold flavor, Batangas coffee is a must-try for coffee enthusiasts. Grown in the fertile volcanic soil of the region, it offers a unique taste that reflects the local terroir.

Suman

A traditional Filipino rice cake wrapped in banana leaves, suman is a sweet delicacy made from glutinous rice and coconut milk. It's often served during special occasions and celebrations.

Bulalo

A hearty beef soup made from beef shanks and bone marrow, bulalo is a comforting dish that showcases the region's love for rich, flavorful broths and tender meat.

Kapeng Barako

A strong, aromatic coffee variety native to Batangas, Kapeng Barako is known for its distinctive taste and is often enjoyed black or with minimal sweeteners to appreciate its full flavor.
