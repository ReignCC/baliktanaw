Food, Delicacy, Cuisines

Lomi

Lomi is a type of noodle soup dish that makes use of thick egg noodles.

Gotong Batangas

Goto usually refers to rice porridge in Filipino.

Tawilis

Sardinella tawilis is a freshwater sardine found exclusively in the Philippines.

Adobo sa Dilaw

Adobong Dilaw is a pork adobo version that does not use soy sauce.

Native Kakanin

Kakanin is a term for various native snacks and desserts in the Philippines.

Puto Bao

Puto Bao is a steamed rice cake using bao ng niyog (coconut husks) as molders.

Atchara

Atchara or Atcharang Papaya is an appetizer made from pickled green papaya.

Kapeng Barako

Kapeng barako, also known as Barako coffee or Batangas coffee, is a coffee varietal grown in the Philippines.
