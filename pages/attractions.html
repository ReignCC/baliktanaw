Must-Visit Attractions

Tanauan City Plaza

The heart of Tanauan City, this central plaza serves as a gathering place for locals and visitors alike. It features beautiful landscaping, historical monuments, and is perfect for leisurely strolls and community events.

City Center Open 24/7

Mount Maculot

A popular hiking destination offering breathtaking views of Taal Lake and the surrounding landscape. The mountain features different trails suitable for various skill levels, making it perfect for adventure seekers.

Cuenca, Batangas Best: 6AM-5PM

Taal Lake Viewpoints

Several scenic spots around Tanauan offer spectacular views of the iconic Taal Lake and Volcano. These viewpoints are perfect for photography, relaxation, and appreciating the natural beauty of the region.

Various Locations Sunrise/Sunset

Parks and Gardens

Tanauan boasts several well-maintained parks and gardens that provide peaceful retreats for visitors. These green spaces offer walking paths, playgrounds, and areas for outdoor activities and family gatherings.

Multiple Locations 6AM-10PM
